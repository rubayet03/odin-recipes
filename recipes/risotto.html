Risotto

Description

Indulge in the creamy goodness of a classic risotto, a comforting Italian dish that's as versatile as it is delicious. Follow our step-by-step guide to create a perfect bowl of creamy risotto that will tantalize your taste buds and impress your guests.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth, kept warm
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Steps

  1. In a large skillet or saucepan, heat olive oil and 1 tablespoon of butter over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onions and garlic, saut√© until translucent.
  3. Add Arborio rice to the pan, stirring to coat the rice with the oil and butter mixture.
  4. Pour in the white wine, stirring constantly until the wine is mostly absorbed.
  5. Begin adding the warm broth, one ladle at a time, stirring frequently. Allow the liquid to be absorbed before adding more broth. Continue this process until the rice is creamy and cooked to al dente texture.
  6. Stir in the remaining butter and grated Parmesan cheese. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  7. Remove from heat and let it rest for a couple of minutes. The risotto should be creamy and slightly soupy.
  8. Serve the risotto in bowls, garnished with fresh parsley.
