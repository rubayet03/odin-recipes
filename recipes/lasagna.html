Lasagna

Description

There's nothing quite like the rich and savory layers of a classic homemade lasagna. This timeless Italian dish is a favorite for family gatherings and cozy dinners. In this recipe, we'll guide you through the steps to create a mouthwatering lasagna that will have everyone coming back for seconds.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef
  • 1 pound (450g) Italian sausage, casings removed
  • large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cans (28 ounces each) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans (6 ounces each) tomato paste
  • 2 cans (15 ounces each) tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup red wine (optional)
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 9 lasagna noodles, cooked according to package instructions
  • 4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil or parsley for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef and Italian sausage over medium heat. Drain excess fat.
  2. Add chopped onions and minced garlic to the skillet. Sauté until the onions are softened.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, and red wine (if using).
  4. Add dried basil, dried oregano, sugar, salt, and pepper. Simmer the sauce for about 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  6. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions until al dente.
  7. Drain and set aside, separating the noodles to prevent sticking.
  8. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  9. In a greased 9x13-inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce.
  10. Place three lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.
  11. Spread a layer of ricotta cheese over the noodles, followed by a layer of shredded mozzarella.
  12. Repeat the layers, finishing with a layer of meat sauce on top.
  13. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese over the top.
  14. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
  15. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  16. Allow the lasagna to cool for a few minutes before slicing.
  17. Garnish with fresh basil or parsley if desired.
  18. Serve with a side salad and garlic bread for a complete meal.
